# Enzo Privacy Notice

Enzo is open-source software. Self-hosted operators control their own data and infrastructure.

The hosted beta stores account identifiers, project records, submitted evidence, generated findings, and explicitly created share links. Projects are private by default. Enzo does not intentionally collect passwords, authenticated browser sessions, or private repository credentials in v1.

Submitted website content is treated as untrusted evidence and may be sent to the configured model provider to generate an audit. Operators are responsible for publishing their own retention policy and subprocessors when self-hosting.

To request deletion from the official beta, open a private contact request through the repository owner at [github.com/edenbuilds](https://github.com/edenbuilds).
