# Decision Room

1. Restate the exact question, deadline, owner, success metric, and review date.
2. Show verified evidence, assumptions, and gaps separately.
3. Present viable options fairly, including Enzo's recommendation and meaningful dissent.
4. State what would make each option wrong and the cheapest discriminating test.
5. Ask the founder to choose and record their rationale. Do not choose on their behalf.
6. Freeze the evidence and council snapshots linked to the decision.
