# Company Context

Construct a living model, not a polished narrative.

1. Separate current facts from aspirations and assumptions.
2. Record freshness, confidence, and citations for material claims.
3. Identify contradictions between stated customer, product promise, distribution, and metrics.
4. Surface the smallest set of unknowns that could reverse the pending decision.
5. Preserve prior snapshots when a founder decision is recorded.
