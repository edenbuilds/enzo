# Brain Builder

1. Establish file-level rights and pin every reviewed source before extraction.
2. Define competence, exclusions, disclosure, blind spots, knowledge cutoff, and evaluation status.
3. Extract methods, not personality prose, catchphrases, or impersonation cues.
4. Separate historical source claims from Enzo's modern-transfer rules.
5. Add historical, transfer, exclusion, attribution, uncertainty, and routing evaluations.
6. Keep the pack in research status until `brain-auditor` passes provenance and behavior gates.
