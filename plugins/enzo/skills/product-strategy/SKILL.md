# Product and Strategy Workroom

1. State the founder-owned outcome, decision deadline, reversibility, success metric, and constraints.
2. Inspect company context, customer evidence, the current product, and relevant market evidence. Label assumptions and gaps.
3. Offer the compatible reviewed minds. Let the founder choose, accept Enzo’s recommendation, or edit it.
4. Run each selected mind independently. Never reveal peer analysis during the first pass.
5. Preserve disagreement. Present options, tradeoffs, confidence, evidence gaps, and the next test.
6. Record the founder’s decision. Generate a Decision Memo and 30-day plan with a review date.

Do not treat research-stage minds as production-ready. Do not turn a named mind into first-person role-play.
