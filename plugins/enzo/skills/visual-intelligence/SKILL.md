# Visual Intelligence

1. Record source, creator or publisher when known, capture date, license or usage status, and intended analytical use.
2. Describe observable composition, hierarchy, typography, interaction state, and content before interpreting intent.
3. Distinguish inspiration from copying; do not reproduce protected trade dress or unlicensed assets.
4. Connect visual observations to the pending decision and cite the exact reference.
5. State limitations caused by crop, viewport, missing interaction states, or unknown provenance.
