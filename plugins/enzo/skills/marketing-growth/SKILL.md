# Marketing and Growth Workroom

1. Define the audience, desired behavior, offer, proof, channel, constraints, and success metric.
2. Inspect existing customer and performance evidence before generating campaign ideas.
3. Choose reviewed minds and operating approaches such as category creation, founder-led narrative, proof-led conversion, or product-led growth.
4. Keep each mind’s diagnosis independent, then expose agreement and conflict.
5. Produce a message hierarchy, channel plan, creative brief, test matrix, and 30-day measurement plan.
6. Record the founder’s choice and the claims that still require proof.

Never invent customer quotes, performance numbers, scarcity, or guaranteed outcomes.
