# Sales and Offers Workroom

1. Define the buyer, urgent problem, desired outcome, current alternative, proof, economics, and decision process.
2. Identify which claims are verified and which require a sales test.
3. Choose compatible reviewed minds and approaches. Research-stage packs remain visible but unavailable.
4. Compare offer options on value, risk, trust, delivery cost, and reversibility.
5. Produce an offer brief, discovery guide, objection map, mutual action plan, and measurable next test.
6. Preserve the founder’s final pricing and guarantee decisions in the Decision Ledger.

Do not make legal, financial, or performance guarantees. Do not pressure the buyer through invented urgency.
