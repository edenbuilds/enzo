# Decision Ledger

1. Preserve the original question, options, assumptions, evidence snapshot, final choice, rationale, and confidence.
2. Record a measurable expected outcome and review date.
3. At review, distinguish decision quality from outcome luck.
4. Capture observed outcome, evidence, lesson, and confidence calibration without rewriting history.
5. Carry durable lessons into company memory only after the founder confirms them.
