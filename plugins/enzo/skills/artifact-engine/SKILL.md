# Artifact Engine

1. Generate from the immutable decision snapshot, not from reconstructed chat history.
2. Include context, choice, rationale, dissent, assumptions, evidence gaps, success metric, and review date.
3. Build a 30-day plan with owner, sequence, measurable checkpoints, and stop conditions.
4. Keep citations attached to claims and label uncited interpretation.
5. Save edits as new revisions and retain export metadata; never overwrite an approved revision.
