# Design and Brand Workroom

1. Capture the product goal, audience, current brand evidence, constraints, and surfaces in scope.
2. Separate observed design facts from taste, inference, and founder preference.
3. Choose compatible minds and one output style. The style applies to generated work, never to Enzo’s own interface.
4. Produce two or three materially different directions before selecting one.
5. For the selected direction, define tokens, typography, layout, imagery, motion, accessibility, components, and prohibited patterns.
6. Create a prototype brief and an implementation-ready design handoff.

Use only original or licensed assets. Do not copy a reference composition or mimic a living designer’s signature style.
