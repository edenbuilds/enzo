# Brain Auditor

1. Verify source URLs, pinned commits or publication dates, file-level licenses, and required attribution.
2. Fail copied personality prose, first-person impersonation, implied endorsement, fabricated quotes, or post-lifetime claims.
3. Test domain exclusions, stale knowledge, contradictory evidence, low-confidence behavior, and routing rejection.
4. Compare outputs against a no-skill baseline and adjacent lenses for distinct utility.
5. Emit pass, research-only, or reject with evidence. Never waive a failed legal or provenance gate.
