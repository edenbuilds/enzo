# Product-promise decision workflow

1. Capture founder constraints and company context.
2. Ingest the current experience and classify claims as verified, user-provided, researched, inferred, assumed, hypothetical, or perspective-specific.
3. Frame “What should this product promise first?” with deadline, reversibility, options, success metric, and review date.
4. Route product-focus, inversion, and customer/operator lenses with explicit rationale and blind spots.
5. Seal three independent analyses containing diagnosis, evidence, challenged assumptions, preserve/remove calls, opportunity, risk, and next action.
6. Run structured disagreement after sealing. Record the evidence that could resolve each dispute.
7. Synthesize shared conclusions, dissent, confidence, evidence gaps, recommendation, and the founder decision required.
8. Record the founder's option and rationale without overwriting dissent.
9. Generate a Decision Memo and 30-day action plan.
10. Review the expected outcome on the recorded date and calibrate confidence.

Invalid transitions fail: outcome before decision, unknown option, mutation of a reviewed decision, unowned records, unsealed council synthesis, or private production input without persistence.
