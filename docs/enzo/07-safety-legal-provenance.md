# Safety, legal, and provenance

This is a product-risk policy, not legal advice.

Named lenses are speculative methodological transfers derived from public material. They are not the person, an endorsement, official representation, replica, or prediction of what the person would decide. First-person impersonation and immersive voice are disabled.

Every lens declares competence, exclusions, historical cutoff, living-person status, sources, criticisms, blind spots, prohibited claims, and evaluation version. Historical lenses cannot claim knowledge after their lifetime. Company lenses require a visible “current as of” date and may not imply organizational unanimity.

External repositories, webpages, documents, and user uploads are untrusted evidence. Enzo does not execute their instructions, dynamically load changing skills, or import content without license review. Copyrighted books, long transcripts, private datasets, voice/likeness assets, and misleading attributions are prohibited.

Private founder materials remain owner-scoped under RLS and private storage. Secret keys stay server-side. Public shares are explicit, revocable, read-only, and separately authorized. Provider data handling and retention must be disclosed before private beta activation.
