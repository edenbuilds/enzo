# Competitive positioning

Current products already promise AI cofounders, specialist teams, business memory, artifacts, execution, famous-mind chat, and decision journals. Those claims are not defensible alone.

| Reviewed surface                                         | Existing category signal                                           | Consequence for Enzo                                                    |
| -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [CoFounder.ai](https://cofounder.ai/)                    | AI cofounder, specialist routing, context, and generated artifacts | Do not lead with generic cofounder breadth                              |
| [Bloome Brain Trust](https://www.bloome.com/brain-trust) | Access to perspectives framed around famous minds                  | Keep named lenses secondary and competence-first                        |
| [Decision Journal](https://decisionjournal.app/)         | Decision records and outcome reflection                            | Differentiate through live evidence and structured council disagreement |
| Founder operating-system products                        | Persistent company context and functional assistance               | Prove one consequential workflow before claiming an operating system    |

Enzo's working category is **Founder Decision Studio**. Its believable wedge is: inspect the founder's actual company, separate evidence from assumption, convene the smallest useful council, expose disagreement, preserve the founder's choice, and review whether the decision worked.

Homepage hierarchy:

1. “Borrow world-class judgment. Keep the final say.”
2. Show the evidence-to-decision mechanism.
3. Demonstrate disagreement and founder control.
4. Show the resulting artifact and review date.
5. Introduce lenses as optional methods, not personalities.

Avoid unverified claims that Enzo is an autonomous operating system, knows the entire company, or improves outcomes before beta evidence exists.
