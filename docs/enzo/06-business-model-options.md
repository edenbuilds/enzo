# Business-model options

Current competitor research shows that “AI cofounder,” specialist routing, memory, and artifact creation are already commercialized, including low-cost subscriptions. Enzo should not price access to public Markdown or named minds.

The recommended beta model is a subscription containing private company memory, a monthly decision allowance, evidence capture, Decision Ledger, and standard domain playbooks. Expensive current research and image analysis are metered as transparent research credits. Named reviewed lenses are included as methodology, not sold as celebrity access.

Future expansion can add advisor collaboration, agency workspaces, private company playbooks, team permissions, and marketplace revenue share. Pricing remains uncommitted until completion rate, model cost per decision, artifact reuse, and outcome-review retention are observed.
