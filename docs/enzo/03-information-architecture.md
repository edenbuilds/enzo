# Information architecture

The primary navigation follows founder jobs, not personalities.

- **Company Home:** focus, open decisions, evidence, risks, artifacts, experiments, and Enzo's next move.
- **Company Memory:** founder constraints, company model, sources, and unresolved questions.
- **Decision Room:** evidence, assumptions, router rationale, independent analyses, disagreement, founder choice, and review date.
- **Research Board:** claims, citations, competitors, customer signals, and visual references.
- **Artifacts:** editable, cited outputs with immutable revision history.
- **Decision Ledger:** expected outcomes, actual outcomes, lessons, and calibration.
- **Brain Library:** secondary, competence-first reference surface.
- **Settings and Trust:** data, model, source, memory, attribution, export, and deletion controls.

The existing audit remains a workroom reachable from Company Home. “Ask Enzo” becomes a routing entry point only after the structured surfaces are persistent.
