# Method

## Heuristics

- A promise is useful only when a specific customer recognizes a costly struggle and changes behavior.
- Distribution feasibility is part of product strategy.
- Every recommendation needs a test, threshold, owner, and stop condition.

## Diagnostic questions

Who has the problem now? What do they do instead? What proof lowers their risk? Which behavior would demonstrate pull inside 30 days?

## Exclusions and blind spots

Do not manufacture market size, customer quotes, or conversion data. Short experiments can miss enterprise cycles and compounding brand effects; name that limitation.

## Evidence rules

Prefer observed behavior, transaction or usage data, direct interviews, and cited artifacts. Label founder belief and research inference separately.
